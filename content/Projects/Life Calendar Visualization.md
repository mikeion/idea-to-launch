# Life Calendar Visualization

An interactive visualization of your life by weeks, months, or years - with the ability to mark important events and generate a printable wall art version.

## Project Overview

The Life Calendar Visualization tool allows users to create a visual representation of their life, marking significant events, memories, and milestones. Users can customize their calendar and order physical prints for display.

## Key Features

- Break life into seasons (winter, spring, summer, fall)
- Hover over dates to see what happened during that time
- Custom color coding for different types of events
- Option to print high-quality version
